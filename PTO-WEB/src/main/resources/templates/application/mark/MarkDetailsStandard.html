A transliteration of a trademark is the phonetic spelling, in the standard English alphabet, of the word(s) in the trademark that are in non-Latin characters. For example, if your trademark includes Chinese characters, the transliteration will state how those characters will be spelled out based on how they sound, using the English alphabet.
